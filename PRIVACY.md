# Meetrics – Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 24, 2026**

## Overview

Meetrics is a personal data tracking app. Your privacy is important. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is
used, and your rights.

---

## Data You Enter

All data you log in Meetrics — tags, values, dates, and notes — is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's
SwiftData framework. This data is never transmitted to any server by Meetrics, and is not accessible to the developer.

---

## AI Analyst Feature (Premium)

When you use the Analyst chat feature, your messages and a summary of your data context are sent to a third-party AI
provider to generate responses. This data is used solely to answer your query and is not stored or used to train models
beyond the provider's standard terms of service.

The Analyst feature is opt-in and only available to Premium subscribers.

---

## Notifications

If you grant notification permissions, Meetrics schedules a daily local reminder on your device. No notification data is
transmitted to any server.

---

## HealthKit

If you connect HealthKit, Meetrics reads health data solely to display it within the app. This data is never uploaded or
shared.

---

## Subscriptions

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. Meetrics does not collect or store any payment information.

---

## Analytics

Meetrics does not use any third-party analytics SDKs. Aggregate, anonymised usage statistics may be available to the
developer through Apple's App Store Connect platform, subject to Apple's privacy policies.

---

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply delete the app from your device. Since all data is stored locally, deletion is immediate
and complete.

---

## Children

Meetrics is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
